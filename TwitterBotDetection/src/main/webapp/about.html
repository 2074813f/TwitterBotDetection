About

Recently the discovery of large networks of automated users (bots) have been investigated over their influence on the social media platform Twitter. The "Twitter Bot Detection" project allows users to identify bots or humans using automated classification of Twitter accounts. The project utilises machine learning algorithms to produce a model for classifying users. Twitter accounts and statuses are retrieved and cached to produce a raw dataset from which a number of features used to identify bot-like behaviour are generated. After implementation of the classifier and production of a user interface to demonstrate the capabilities of the system, experiments were conducted to evaluate several aspects of the system, recognising considerations for feature transformation and limitations of the training dataset.
